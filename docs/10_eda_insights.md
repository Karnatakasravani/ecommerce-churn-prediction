\# Exploratory Data Analysis Insights



---



\## 1. Customer Distribution

\- Majority customers are from the UK

\- Top 5 countries account for ~80% revenue

\- Long-tail international presence



---



\## 2. Churn Rate

\- Overall churn ≈ X%

\- Consistent across regions

\- Frequent buyers churn less



---



\## 3. Recency Behaviour

\- Strong right-skew

\- Many customers inactive for long periods

\- Predictive signal confirmed



---



\## 4. Frequency Behaviour

\- Majority make ≤ 3 purchases

\- Heavy buyers rare but valuable



---



\## 5. Monetary Behaviour

\- Revenue highly skewed

\- Small % of customers contribute majority of revenue (Pareto effect)



---



\## 6. Correlations

\- Recency ↑ → Churn ↑

\- Frequency ↑ → Churn ↓

\- Monetary ↑ → Churn ↓



---



\## 7. Key Observations

✔ Loyal customers purchase frequently  

✔ High-value buyers churn less  

✔ Single-purchase users churn fastest  



---



\## 8. Data Risks

\- Outliers due to bulk buying

\- Seasonality effects

\- International behaviour differences



---



\## 9. Business Insights

\- Target frequent buyers early

\- Retain high-value users first

\- Offer re-activation campaigns
